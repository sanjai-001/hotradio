Suriya Sivakumar, commonly known as Suriya, is a prominent Indian actor who primarily works in Tamil cinema. Born on July 23, 1975, he is the son of the renowned actor Sivakumar. Suriya made his acting debut in 1997 with the film *Nerukku Ner* and quickly gained recognition for his versatility and dedication to his craft. He has starred in numerous successful films across various genres, showcasing his range from intense dramas to action-packed thrillers. Notable movies in his career include *Ghajini*, *Singam*, and *Soorarai Pottru*, the latter of which has earned critical acclaim for its portrayal of a self-made entrepreneur. Suriya's performances often receive praise for their depth and authenticity, and he has won several awards for his work, including multiple Filmfare Awards. Beyond acting, Suriya is known for his philanthropic efforts, particularly through the Agaram Foundation, which supports underprivileged children’s education. His contributions to cinema and society have established him as a respected figure both on and off the screen.
