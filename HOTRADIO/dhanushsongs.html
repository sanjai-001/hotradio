Dhanush, born Venkatesh Prabhu Kasthuri Raja, is a versatile Indian actor, producer, director, and playback singer primarily known for his work in Tamil cinema. Rising to fame with his unique screen presence and natural acting style, Dhanush has captivated audiences with his performances across various genres. He made his acting debut in 2002 with the film Thulluvadho Ilamai, directed by his father Kasthuri Raja. Dhanush quickly became a household name with hits like Kadhal Kondein, Pudhupettai, and Aadukalam, the latter earning him a National Film Award for Best Actor. Beyond Tamil cinema, Dhanush gained international recognition with his song "Why This Kolaveri Di," which became a global sensation. He also made a successful foray into Bollywood with films like Raanjhanaa and Atrangi Re. Dhanush's ability to portray a wide range of characters with depth and authenticity has earned him critical acclaim and a massive fan following.
